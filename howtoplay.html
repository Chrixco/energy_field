How To Play



Let's Explore

Discover the essentials of gameplay and embark on a journey of strategic invention.

Winning Condition

The goal is to amass the most victory points through strategic card placement and inventive combinations. Points are tallied at game end, triggered by one of two conditions:

  • 1) The Inventorâ€™s Deck runs out of cards, preventing further draws.
  • 2) The Invention Zone is closed, leaving no room for additional cards.

Gameplay Overview

Gameplay unfolds in turns, each consisting of distinct phases designed to strategically advance players towards victory:

  1. Setup: Players place their Inventors in the Invention Zone, draw five cards each, and let's beginn!
  2. Draw Phase: The player in turn, draws a card from each deck. (Inventor's Deck and Energy Deck)
  3. Play Phase: Player may deploy Energies, Circuits, Tools, and Inventions, each contributing uniquely to their network. Constraints apply: one Circuit, one Energy per turn, unlimited Inventions and Tools.
  4. Ability Use: Activate Inventor and Tool abilities once per turn by fulfilling energy costs.

Engage in this cycle of creativity and strategy, navigating through the flow of Energy Field, as you aim for the title of Inventor.
