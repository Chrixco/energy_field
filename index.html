Card Types

Energy Field is a turn-based game, not combat based, that uses five types of cards: Energies, Circuits, Inventors, Tools and Inventions to create a colective network with your opponent. Each piece has a unique function and abilities, making the game challenging and exciting every time you play..

  • Inventors

    Inventor cards are essential components of the game, symbolizing your presence and assisting in shaping your strategic approach during gameplay.

  • Tools

    Tools have special abbilities you can use once per turn by paying the activation cost with Energy.

  • Inventions

    Inventions have unique victory points, costs, effects and characteristics. Play them by paying the energy cost

  • Energy

    Energy allow us to play Inventions or activate abilities from Tools and Inventors.

  • Circuits

    Circuits represent possible paths between Inventors and their Inventions in the game.
